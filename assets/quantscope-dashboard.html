One lens on price. One lens on value.

QuantScope pairs a machine-learning market direction classifier with a fixed-income valuation and yield-forecasting engine — the two sides of how quants actually think: where is the price going, and what is the cash flow worth. Built on live Yahoo Finance data and Indian G-Sec yield history.

Bond inputs

DCF (discounted cash flow): every future coupon and the final principal repayment is discounted back to today at the market yield — the sum is the bond's fair price. Duration: the weighted-average time to receive the bond's cash flows, and a first-order measure of interest-rate risk.
